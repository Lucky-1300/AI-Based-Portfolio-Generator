## Getting Started

### Accessing the Application

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to `http://localhost:3000` (or your deployment URL)
3. You'll see the Home page with login/signup options

### Signing Up

1. Click **"Sign Up"** button
2. Fill in the following information:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm password
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Check your email for a verification link
5. Click the verification link to activate your account

### Logging In

1. Click **"Login"** button
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

## Creating Your Portfolio

### Step 1: Basic Information

1. Click **"Create New Portfolio"** on the Dashboard
2. Enter your name and professional title
3. Add your email and phone number
4. Write a brief bio (2-3 sentences about your professional background)
5. Upload a profile photo (optional)
6. Click **"Next"**

### Step 2: Professional Experience

1. Click **"Add Experience"**
2. Fill in the following details:
   - Company name
   - Job title
   - Start date and end date
   - Description of your responsibilities and achievements
3. To enhance with AI, click **"AI Enhance"** for suggestions
4. Click **"Save"** to add the experience
5. Repeat to add more experiences
6. Click **"Next"**

### Step 3: Skills

1. Click **"Add Skill"** or use the skill suggestions
2. Enter skill name and select proficiency level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert)
3. Add relevant keywords/tags
4. Click **"Save"**
5. Add 5-15 skills for better portfolio quality
6. Click **"Next"**

### Step 4: Education

1. Click **"Add Education"**
2. Fill in:
   - School/University name
   - Degree and field of study
   - Graduation date
   - Description (optional)
3. Click **"Save"**
4. Add all relevant education
5. Click **"Next"**

### Step 5: Projects

1. Click **"Add Project"**
2. Fill in project details:
   - Project title
   - Description
   - Technologies used
   - Project link (optional)
   - Start and end dates
3. Upload project screenshots (optional)
4. Click **"Save"**
5. Add 3-5 showcase projects
6. Click **"Next"**

### Step 6: Review and Publish

1. Preview your complete portfolio
2. Review all sections
3. Click **"Publish"** to make your portfolio live
4. You'll receive a public URL for your portfolio

## Using AI Enhancement

### What Can AI Help With?

The AI enhancement feature helps you:
- Craft compelling professional descriptions
- Identify important keywords for each role
- Generate professional summaries
- Suggest skill categorization
- Optimize your content for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)

### How to Use AI Enhancement

#### For Experience Descriptions

1. Click the **"✨ AI Enhance"** button next to an experience entry
2. Review the AI-generated suggestions
3. Select options you like or modify them
4. Click **"Apply"** to update your experience
5. You can request alternative versions

#### For Professional Summary

1. Go to the **"Summary"** section
2. Click **"Generate with AI"**
3. Select your desired tone:
   - Professional
   - Friendly
   - Technical
4. Review generated options
5. Select your favorite or customize it
6. Click **"Save"**

#### For Skills Matching

1. Add your skills
2. Click **"Smart Categorize"**
3. AI will automatically categorize skills by type
4. Review and adjust as needed

### AI Tips

- Be specific in your descriptions for better AI suggestions
- Review and personalize all AI-generated content
- Use the "Alternative Suggestions" feature to explore different wordings
- The more information you provide, the better the AI recommendations

## Customization

### Choosing a Theme

1. Click **"Theme"** in the navigation
2. Select from available themes:
   - **Modern**: Contemporary design with clean lines
   - **Minimal**: Simple and elegant layout
   - **Classic**: Traditional professional look
3. Preview the changes
4. Click **"Apply Theme"**

### Color Customization

1. Go to **"Customize"** → **"Colors"**
2. Choose a color scheme or create a custom one:
   - Primary color
   - Secondary color
   - Accent color
3. Click **"Preview"** to see changes in real-time
4. Click **"Save"**

### Font Selection

1. Go to **"Customize"** → **"Typography"**
2. Select fonts for headings and body text
3. Adjust font sizes
4. Preview changes
5. Click **"Apply"**

### Section Reordering

1. Go to **"Edit Layout"**
2. Drag sections to reorder them:
   - About/Summary
   - Experience
   - Skills
   - Projects
   - Education
3. Toggle sections on/off to hide/show them
4. Click **"Save Layout"**

### Custom CSS

For advanced users:

1. Go to **"Advanced"** → **"Custom CSS"**
2. Add your CSS rules
3. Preview in real-time
4. Click **"Save"**

## Exporting Your Portfolio

### Export as PDF

1. Click **"Export"** button
2. Select **"PDF"**
3. Choose a template:
   - Modern
   - Minimal
   - Classic
4. Click **"Download PDF"**
5. The PDF will download to your computer

**Best Practices:**
- Use for submitting to job applications
- One-page or two-page format available
- Fully formatted and print-ready

### Export as HTML

1. Click **"Export"** button
2. Select **"HTML"**
3. Choose a template
4. Click **"Download HTML"**
5. Open the HTML file in any browser

**Use Cases:**
- Self-hosting your portfolio
- Customizing with your own web host
- Creating a static website

### Export as React Component

1. Click **"Export"** button
2. Select **"React Component"**
3. Choose a template
4. Click **"Download JSX"**
5. Integrate into your React project

**Requirements:**
- Basic React knowledge
- npm/yarn installed
- Tailwind CSS or custom styling

### Share Public Link

1. Click **"Share"** button
2. Copy your public portfolio link
3. Share via:
   - Email
   - LinkedIn
   - Twitter
   - Any messaging platform

## FAQ

### Q: Can I use my portfolio for multiple job applications?
**A:** Yes! You can create multiple versions of your portfolio tailored to different industries or roles.

### Q: How often can I update my portfolio?
**A:** Update as often as you want. Changes appear immediately on your public profile.

### Q: Is my portfolio private?
**A:** Portfolios are public by default. You can make yours private in Settings.

### Q: Can I delete sections?
**A:** Yes, in the Layout section, toggle off any section you don't want to display.

### Q: How do I change my password?
**A:** Go to Settings → Security → Change Password.

### Q: Can I download my data?
**A:** Yes, go to Settings → Data → Export My Data.

### Q: What file formats are supported for uploads?
**A:** Images: JPG, PNG (max 5MB); Project files: PDF, ZIP.

### Q: How long does AI enhancement take?
**A:** Usually 2-5 seconds. Premium users get faster processing.

### Q: Can I use my portfolio offline?
**A:** Download the HTML version to use offline or with your own server.

### Q: Is there a limit to portfolio entries?
**A:** Standard: 50 entries per section. Premium: Unlimited.

### Q: How do I contact support?
**A:** Email: support@portfoliogenerator.com or use the in-app chat.

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Use keywords relevant to your industry** - Helps with searchability and ATS
2. **Add quantifiable achievements** - Use numbers and percentages
3. **Keep descriptions concise** - 2-3 bullet points per experience
4. **Update regularly** - Keep your portfolio current
5. **Use high-quality photos** - Professional profile picture recommended
6. **Test your links** - Ensure all project links work
7. **Customize for each application** - Tailor your summary and top skills
